1. Purpose

The purpose of this Agreement is to define the monthly incentive structure payable by the Company to the Dealer for securing solar installation orders, based on the total project capacity (kW) achieved within a calendar month.

2. Monthly Incentive Structure

At the end of each month, the total installed/commissioned capacity achieved by the Dealer will be calculated. Incentives will be paid according to the following slabs:
● For the first systems of kW, your commission will be ₹ per system.
● If the total installations exceed 5 to 10 systems of 3kw, your commission will be ₹12,000 per system for all additional systems.
● If the total installations exceed 11 systems of 3kw, your commission will be ₹15,000 per system for all additional systems.

3. Sales Target

• The Dealer is expected to achieve a minimum sales target of 6 kW per month.
• If the Dealer fails to meet the monthly target for two consecutive months, the Company reserves the right to:
- Review or revise the incentive rates, OR
- Suspend incentive payments, OR
- Terminate this Agreement.

4. Payment Terms

• Incentives will be calculated on the basis of the total commissioned capacity per month.
• Payment will be released to the Dealer within 30 days of month-end, subject to full payment collection from customers.
• All applicable statutory deductions (e.g., TDS) will apply.

5. Obligations of the Dealer

• Promote Company products in the designated area.
• Ensure genuine customer orders and accurate documentation.
• Follow Company policies, product guidelines, and government regulations.

6. Term and Termination

• This Agreement shall remain valid for one year from the date of signing, unless extended in writing by both Parties.
• Either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving days’ written notice.

6. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
